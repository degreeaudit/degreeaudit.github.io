HEC Attestation Fees: Updated Costs and Payment Methods

Home / Blog / Blog Details

Navigating the world of document attestation can be a daunting task, especially when it involves international education or employment. The Higher Education Commission (HEC) of Pakistan plays a pivotal role in verifying academic credentials, ensuring their authenticity for global recognition. With rising demand for overseas opportunities, understanding the latest HEC attestation fees and payment methods is crucial for students and professionals alike.

Why HEC Attestation Matters in Today’s Global Landscape

In an era where remote work and cross-border education are booming, verified academic documents are non-negotiable. Whether you’re applying for a student visa, seeking employment abroad, or pursuing higher studies, HEC attestation serves as a stamp of legitimacy. Countries like the UAE, Canada, and the UK require attested degrees to prevent fraud and maintain educational standards.

The Impact of Digitalization on Attestation

The HEC has streamlined its processes with online portals, reducing physical visits and paperwork. However, fees and payment methods have evolved, and staying updated ensures a hassle-free experience.

Breakdown of HEC Attestation Fees (2024 Update)

The cost of HEC attestation varies depending on the type of document and urgency. Below is a detailed fee structure:

1. Degree Attestation Fees

  • Normal Processing (15 working days): PKR 2,000 per document
  • Urgent Processing (7 working days): PKR 4,000 per document

2. Transcript Attestation Fees

  • Normal Processing: PKR 1,500 per document
  • Urgent Processing: PKR 3,000 per document

3. Additional Charges

  • Duplicate Degree Attestation: PKR 3,500
  • Verification from Foreign Universities: PKR 5,000 (varies by institution)

Note: Fees are subject to change based on HEC policies. Always check the official website before applying.

Accepted Payment Methods for HEC Attestation

Gone are the days of standing in long queues to pay fees. The HEC now offers multiple payment options:

1. Online Payment via HEC Portal

  • Credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard)
  • JazzCash and EasyPaisa
  • Bank transfers through designated banks (Allied Bank, Habib Bank)

2. Over-the-Counter Payments

  • Visit authorized branches of Allied Bank Limited (ABL) and submit the fee voucher.

3. Mobile Wallets

  • Services like JazzCash and EasyPaisa allow instant payments using the HEC’s reference code.

How Rising Inflation Affects Attestation Costs

With Pakistan’s inflation rate soaring, government fees, including attestation, have seen incremental hikes. In 2021, the normal attestation fee was PKR 1,000—now it’s doubled. This reflects broader economic challenges, making budget planning essential for applicants.

Tips to Save on Attestation Costs

  • Apply early to avoid urgent processing fees.
  • Double-check documents to prevent rejections and re-submission charges.
  • Use online payment methods to save time and transportation costs.

The Role of HEC Attestation in Combating Degree Fraud

Fake degrees remain a global concern, with scandals like the Axact case highlighting the need for strict verification. HEC attestation acts as a safeguard, ensuring only legitimate credentials are recognized internationally.

How Blockchain Could Revolutionize Attestation

Countries like Malta and Singapore are adopting blockchain for credential verification. If HEC integrates similar technology, attestation could become instantaneous and even more secure.

Common Challenges and How to Overcome Them

Delays in Processing

  • Solution: Apply well in advance and opt for urgent processing if needed.

Payment Failures

  • Solution: Ensure sufficient balance and correct reference codes when paying online.

Document Rejections

  • Solution: Verify all details match your original certificates before submission.

Future Trends in Academic Verification

As AI and automation advance, expect faster, more transparent attestation processes. The HEC may also introduce flat-rate fees or subscription models for frequent applicants.

For now, staying informed about fees and payment methods is the key to a smooth attestation journey. Whether you’re a student aiming for a foreign scholarship or a professional expanding your career globally, HEC attestation remains a critical step in your path to success.
