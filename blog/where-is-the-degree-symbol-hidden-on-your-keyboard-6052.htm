Where Is the Degree Symbol Hidden on Your Keyboard?

Home / Blog / Blog Details

In today’s fast-paced digital world, where climate change, global temperatures, and scientific data dominate headlines, one tiny symbol holds surprising significance: the degree symbol (°). Whether you're discussing rising temperatures, calculating angles in engineering, or simply texting about the weather, knowing how to type this elusive character can save time and frustration. But where exactly is it hiding on your keyboard?

The Mystery of the Missing Degree Symbol

Unlike common symbols like the dollar sign ($) or the percent sign (%), the degree symbol isn’t always visible on standard keyboards. Its absence can be puzzling, especially when you need it for professional reports, academic papers, or social media posts about record-breaking heatwaves.

Why the Degree Symbol Matters More Than Ever

With climate change accelerating, the degree symbol has become a critical part of global conversations. A single degree Celsius can mean the difference between a habitable planet and catastrophic weather events. Scientists, journalists, and activists rely on this symbol to communicate precise data—yet many still struggle to find it on their devices.

How to Type the Degree Symbol on Different Devices

On Windows PCs

  1. Alt Code Method: Hold down the Alt key and type 0176 on the numeric keypad. Release Alt, and the ° symbol will appear.
  2. Character Map: Search for "Character Map" in the Start menu, locate the degree symbol, and copy-paste it.
  3. Keyboard Shortcut: Some keyboards allow Alt + Shift + 8 or Ctrl + Shift + @ followed by a space.

On Mac Computers

  1. Keyboard Shortcut: Press Option + Shift + 8.
  2. Emoji & Symbols Menu: Use Control + Command + Space to open the character viewer and search for "degree."

On Smartphones (iOS & Android)

  1. iOS: Hold the 0 key to reveal the ° symbol.
  2. Android: Switch to the symbols keyboard (usually under ?123 or =\<) and look for °.

The Degree Symbol in a Warming World

As global temperatures rise, the degree symbol has evolved from a niche mathematical notation to a symbol of urgency. Here’s how it’s being used today:

Climate Reports and Scientific Research

From IPCC reports to local weather forecasts, the degree symbol is everywhere. A single misplaced or missing symbol can alter the interpretation of critical data.

Social Media and Activism

Activists use the degree symbol to highlight temperature extremes. Hashtags like #1Point5Degrees and #KeepItCool rely on this tiny character to convey big messages.

Everyday Communication

Whether you’re texting a friend about a 90°F day or posting about a -40°C polar vortex, the degree symbol ensures clarity.

Why Isn’t the Degree Symbol More Accessible?

Given its importance, why isn’t the degree symbol as easy to type as a comma or period? The answer lies in keyboard design history. Early typewriters prioritized common punctuation, and modern keyboards inherited those layouts. However, as climate discourse grows, perhaps it’s time for a redesign.

Final Thoughts

Next time you’re typing about the weather, coding a project, or drafting a climate report, remember: the degree symbol might be hidden, but its impact is undeniable. With these shortcuts, you’ll never have to search for it again.
