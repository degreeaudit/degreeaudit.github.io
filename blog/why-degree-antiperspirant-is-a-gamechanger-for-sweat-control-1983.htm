The Science Behind Sweat and Why It’s a Modern Struggle

The Biology of Sweating

Sweat is our body’s built-in cooling system, but for many, it’s an overactive one. Whether it’s due to genetics, stress, or climate change-induced rising temperatures, excessive sweating (hyperhidrosis) affects millions globally. Traditional deodorants mask odor, but antiperspirants like Degree go a step further—they actually block sweat at the source.

Climate Change and Rising Body Temperatures

With global temperatures hitting record highs, staying dry is harder than ever. Heatwaves are becoming the norm, and our sweat glands are working overtime. Degree’s advanced sweat-activated technology adapts to your body’s needs, offering stronger protection when you need it most.

Dermatologist-Tested and Gentle on Skin
