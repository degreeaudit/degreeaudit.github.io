Why Oily Skin Needs Special Attention

Oily skin isn’t just a cosmetic issue—it’s a biological one. Excess sebum production can clog pores, attract dirt, and create a breeding ground for bacteria. When combined with sweat, this can lead to unpleasant odors and even skin irritation. Traditional deodorants often fall short because they either don’t control oil effectively or leave a sticky residue.

The Science Behind Oily Skin and Sweat

Sebaceous glands are more active in people with oily skin, producing excess oil that mixes with sweat. This combination not only makes you feel greasy but also amplifies body odor. Degree Men's Deodorant is formulated with advanced odor-fighting technology and oil-absorbing ingredients to keep you fresh without clogging pores.

Simply applying deodorant isn’t enough—you need a strategic routine to keep oily skin in check.
