Why Staying Dry Matters More Than Ever

From rising global temperatures to the increasing demands of hybrid work environments, men today face unique challenges when it comes to staying fresh. Sweat isn’t just about discomfort—it can impact your performance, your interactions, and even your mental well-being.

The Science of Sweat and Stress

Stress sweat is real. When cortisol levels spike—whether from a tight deadline or a heated debate—your body reacts by producing more sweat, often with a stronger odor. Traditional deodorants might mask the smell, but only a high-performance antiperspirant like Degree Men’s can tackle the root cause: wetness itself.

Climate Change and the Need for Advanced Protection

With record-breaking heatwaves becoming the norm, staying dry isn’t just about comfort—it’s about health. Excessive sweating can lead to dehydration, skin irritation, and even heat exhaustion. Degree’s 48-hour protection and motion-activated technology ensure you’re covered, whether you’re commuting in scorching heat or pushing through an intense workout.
