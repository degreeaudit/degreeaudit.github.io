UTSA Degree Works: How to Stay Motivated Toward Your Degree

Home / Blog / Blog Details

Pursuing a degree is one of the most rewarding yet challenging journeys you’ll ever undertake. Whether you're a freshman just starting or a senior nearing graduation, staying motivated can be tough—especially in a world filled with distractions, economic uncertainties, and rapidly evolving career landscapes. At UTSA, Degree Works is a powerful tool to help you track your progress, but motivation must come from within. Here’s how to keep your eyes on the prize and push through to graduation.

The Modern Student’s Struggle: Why Motivation Fades

The Pressure of a Fast-Changing World

Today’s students face unprecedented challenges. The job market is shifting due to AI, automation, and globalization. Many traditional career paths are disappearing, while new ones emerge overnight. This uncertainty can make it hard to stay focused on your degree when the future feels unpredictable.

Financial Stress and Student Debt

With rising tuition costs and student loan debt at an all-time high, it’s easy to question whether a degree is worth the investment. Balancing work, school, and personal life adds another layer of stress, making burnout a real threat.

Digital Distractions and Short Attention Spans

Social media, streaming services, and endless notifications compete for your attention. The average attention span has shrunk, making sustained focus on long-term goals like a degree more difficult than ever.

How UTSA Degree Works Can Help

UTSA’s Degree Works is more than just a degree audit tool—it’s a roadmap to success. Here’s how to use it effectively to stay on track:

Visualizing Your Progress

Seeing your completed courses and remaining requirements in a clear, color-coded format can be incredibly motivating. Each time you check Degree Works, you’re reminded of how far you’ve come and what’s left to accomplish.

Setting Short-Term Milestones

Instead of fixating on graduation (which may feel far away), break your degree into smaller, manageable chunks. Use Degree Works to identify which classes you need each semester and celebrate completing each one.

Exploring "What-If" Scenarios

If you’re feeling stuck or unsure about your major, Degree Works allows you to explore alternative degree paths without derailing your progress. This flexibility can reignite your passion for learning.

Strategies to Stay Motivated Beyond the Tool

Connect Your Degree to Real-World Impact

Ask yourself: How will my degree help solve today’s biggest problems? Whether it’s climate change, social justice, or technological innovation, aligning your studies with a greater purpose can fuel your drive.

Build a Support Network

Surround yourself with peers, mentors, and professors who encourage you. Join student organizations, attend networking events, and seek out study groups. You’re not in this alone.

Reward Yourself Along the Way

Small rewards—like treating yourself after acing an exam or taking a weekend off after a tough midterm—can keep you motivated. Just don’t let rewards turn into procrastination traps.

Embrace Flexibility and Self-Compassion

Some semesters will be harder than others. If you face setbacks—whether it’s a failed class or personal struggles—remember that progress isn’t linear. Adjust your plan, seek help, and keep moving forward.

The Future Belongs to the Persistent

In a world where instant gratification is the norm, earning a degree is a testament to your discipline and vision. By leveraging tools like UTSA Degree Works and adopting a mindset of resilience, you’ll not only graduate—you’ll be prepared to thrive in an ever-changing world.
